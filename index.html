My Outlook for 2021


Never did I think I would graduate from university in the midst of a

global pandemic!

Nevertheless, I am still optimistic for what my future brings post-graduation. This past year has been the most eye-opening for me in terms of I want to do with my life. I picked up a minor in Computer Science two years ago with

zero background

in computer science and absolutely no clue as to what programming entails. My previous career path was to work in the finance industry, but I completely changed course to pursue a more fulfilling and challenging career. I must say it is quite extraordinary to see how far I've come in my journey--blindly learning loops and pointers, staying in the computer lab past midnight to debug my program, and most importantly, overcoming self doubt. Before I knew it, I found clarity in my purpose and aspirations. I used to be daunted by the idea of not making it through to upper-division level courses, but here I am researching new software tools and building small projects as my favorite passtime. I know I still have so much to learn and I'm thrilled to finally go into the "real world" and learn through hands-on industry-level experience.
